Our Services

On Cassava production and supply

Nimakar Farmz Nigeria Enterprises deals in the production and supply of cassava tubers and stems specially on the white cassava known as TME 419. This variety is drought resistant & high yield.

  •    Cassava tuber production
  •    DHQCP (Dried high quality cassava peel).
  •    Garri production
  •    Sales of agro-equipments and tools
  •    Maize production/hybrid Maize & OPV

Off season vegetable productions such as:

  •    Tomato
  •    Pepper
  •    Amarathus app
  •    Cochorus spp
  •    Okra
  •    Watermelon

Supply of Seedlings such as:
